Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?
Click on the "Log in with Blizzard" button and we'll securely send you to Blizzard. This is an official feature, and HSReplay.net never gains access to your password.
Do I need to sign in?
Not at all! Just download Hearthstone Deck Tracker and use it to upload and share your replays. You may claim them later by clicking "Claim Account" in your deck tracker's settings.
How do I share a replay?
Simply send the link to your friends. You can also use the "Share" dialog on the replay page, where you can select on which turn the link should start the replay.
How do you know which cards the opponent was holding in his hand?
Once a card is played from hand we can deduce when that card was drawn, discovered or created in any other way.
